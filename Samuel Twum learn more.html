Samuel Twum's Career and Achievements

Samuel Twum is renowned for his work on large-scale infrastructure projects that have had a lasting impact on Ghana. From the construction of highways and bridges to urban development, his contributions have shaped the country's infrastructure landscape.

Throughout his career, Samuel worked with government agencies and private contractors to develop critical infrastructure, ensuring the safety and sustainability of each project. His legacy is felt through improved transportation networks, better access to essential services, and the promotion of economic growth in Ghana.

Samuel Twum has worked on numerous high-profile projects which include the designing of Aasafo Market, the construction of three bridges, strenghing and widing existing bridges, designing of internal roads in sunyani township inculding dual carriage roads, and various important buildings including church halls. He was also in charge of supervising the construction of structural roads in various townships including quality control.As the Project Manager of SSNIT during his career, he has supervised over 200 projects. He was also the General Manager and was in charge of all Development(Architecture construction and maintainance etc.) and Investment(Bonds, Loans, Bank Overdrafts, etc.) Projects at SSNIT that have played a key role in modernizing Ghana. Samuel's career as a civil engineer began in the early 1990s, and over the years, he honed his skills and expertise, becoming a respected leader in his field.

Samuel’s dedication to engineering excellence and his mentorship of future engineers have made him a respected figure in his field. As a retiree, he continues to influence the next generation, sharing his expertise through speaking engagements and private consultations.

What Samuel Twum Does

Samuel Twum specializes in the planning, designing, and supervising the construction and maintenance of building and infrastructure projects. These projects include a wide range of essential developments such as:

Samuel’s expertise ensures that these projects are completed with the highest standards of safety, sustainability, and efficiency, playing a key role in the advancement of the country's infrastructure.
